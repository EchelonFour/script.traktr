Traktr

Traktr is my fork of script.traktutilities for xbmc, it enables you to sync your xbmc collection with your trakt.tv library, allowing play counts and collection status to be kept up to date. It also scrobbles played TV and movies and allows played items to be rated on trakt from within XBMC.

Main Features

  • Synchronise your movies and tv shows with trakt.tv

  • Scrobbles what you're watching to trakt

  • Can submit both simple and advanced ratings to trakt after what you're watching finishes

  • Supports both XBMC Eden and Frodo
